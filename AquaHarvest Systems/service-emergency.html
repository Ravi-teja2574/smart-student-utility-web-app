Emergency & Disaster Relief

Rapid water deployment when every minute counts.

Critical Crisis Support

When natural disasters strike, water infrastructure is the first to fail. Our mobile units can be airlifted or driven into crisis zones to provide immediate, medical-grade drinking water from the air, requiring no local water source.

  • Fast Setup: Active in under 30 minutes.
  • Mobility: Rugged 4x4 trailer mounted.
  • Reliability: Redundant filtration systems.
