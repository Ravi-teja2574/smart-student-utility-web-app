Solar Retrofitting

Off-grid independence for a sustainable future.

Energy Efficient Harvesting

Our Solar Retrofit kits allow your machines to run 100% on renewable energy. By capturing power during peak sunlight hours, our systems can charge high-capacity batteries to ensure water production continues throughout the night.

Ideal For:

Remote research stations, eco-resorts, and off-grid cabins where traditional power infrastructure is unavailable or unreliable.
